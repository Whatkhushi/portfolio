About Me

🌸

I'm a Computer Science undergraduate at IGDTUW with a passion for creating meaningful impact through technology and community engagement. My journey spans from contributing to open-source projects to mentoring aspiring developers and supporting social causes.

As a GSSoC Contributor and LiFT Scholar, I've had the privilege of working on impactful projects and being part of vibrant tech communities. I believe in the power of mentorship and giving back, which led me to become a mentor under Delhi Government's Desh Ke Mentor program.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me coordinating social outreach activities with Enactus, visiting old age homes and children shelters, or engaging with the community as part of GDG on Campus. I'm always excited to collaborate on projects that combine technology with social good!

Tech Stack: Python, JavaScript, React, Git, HTML/CSS, Node.js, MongoDB
